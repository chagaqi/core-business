Quest 05For the skeptical operator

Questions a sharp founder asks.

?Isn't this just AI slop?
No. The model is a production tool, not the strategist. Every loadout starts from a human hypothesis — a real angle about why a buyer acts — and ends with a human edit and an FTC/Meta-screened claim. The volume is there to test ideas fast, not to flood your account with filler. You greenlight the angles before anything ships.
?Why trust a newer shop?
You shouldn't trust it on faith — that's exactly why the First Strike is free and the guarantee is locked. You see one real ad of your product before you pay, and the first paid cycle has to beat your control or the next one's free. We carry the risk because we're new, not in spite of it.
?How do you make 24 ads off 4 approvals?
The 4 are angles — distinct hypotheses about why someone buys. Each angle becomes 6 production variations: different hooks, openers, edits, and formats testing the same idea. You approve the four directions; the 24 are the disciplined ways we pressure-test each one in the auction.
?How much of my time does this take?
On the Knight (Done-For-You) path, roughly a greenlight call every two weeks. You approve angles and read results; we handle production, launch, tagging, and the read. The whole point of equipping a weapon instead of hiring is that you don't add a workflow.
?What does it cost?
The First Strike is free — no card. The Knight retainer is quoted live against your volume and economics, month-to-month, capped at 5 brands so attention stays high. The Forge (Done-With-You) install is by application. Price isn't the lead; the winner is — we'd rather earn the retainer with a free ad first.
?What if I already run 90 creatives a week?
Then volume isn't your problem — hit rate is. The question isn't how many you ship, it's how many actually beat your control before they fatigue. We're built to raise the rate of winners inside your existing pace, not to add noise to it. The First Strike will show you the difference on one ad.
?Do I own the work — and which vertical is this for?
You own every video, win or lose, on every path. This is built for functional-consumables brands at roughly $20–100K/mo — mushroom coffee, adaptogen gummies, functional beverages, greens, nootropics. It's a deliberately narrow focus: the judgment and economics are tuned to that buyer, not spread thin across everything.
