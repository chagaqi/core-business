The approach

A support layer that understands the emotional arc of a presale buyer

Tideover isn't a new helpdesk. It plugs into the Gorgias, Intercom, or email inbox you already use, reads each order's real production timeline, and drafts a reassurance reply tuned to exactly where that buyer is in the wait. A buyer who's anxious at day 12 needs something completely different from one threatening a dispute at day 89 — and now your support knows the difference.
