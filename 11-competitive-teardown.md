## TL;DR — 10 cross-pattern moves to steal

These showed up in 3+ of the 5 mimic targets. Highest-confidence plays.

1. **Name your method with a number** — M4 Method (Moonlighters), 5 Stages / Quadrant / Hook Formula Matrix (Theriot), G-A-P / Followers-to-Dollars (Dakota), Content Multiplier (Camicees), Build-Teach-Earn (Koe). Memorable IP > clever copy.
2. **Performance guarantee on the front-end** — "Beat your ROAS in 60 days or we work for free" (Theriot), "$50K-or-money-back" (Modern Mastery + Growth Ghost). Risk reversal.
3. **Headline = specific outcome + specific tier** — "Turn 7-fig brands into 8-fig brands" (Moonlighters), "Cut CPAs in 2025, Not Creativity" (mtrx), "Hyper-targeted ad creatives" (Epic Ads Lab). Niched + outcome-promise.
4. **Discovery-call sales for $1K+, public price for entry** — All hide retainer pricing behind a call. Dan Koe is the lone exception (low-ticket subscriptions). Lesson: $497 pack = public, $1.5K+ retainer = call.
5. **Two-tier offer: agency + community/course** — Theriot (agency $10K/mo + Inner Circle $600/mo), Camicees (Epic Ads Lab + Ad Creators Lab on Skool), Moonlighters (agency + The Blueprint Skool). The community is the backend annuity.
6. **Founder-led content is the moat** — Every single one. No exceptions. Cold outreach is the bridge, content is the compounder. Run both for 6 months minimum.
7. **One writing session → repurpose everywhere** — Dan Koe's 30-min morning newsletter → 10-min/day repurposing for X/LI/IG/YT/Threads. Dakota teaches the same.
8. **Specific $+% in every case study** — "43% ROAS in 90 days," "€100K → €270K @ 2.69 ROAS," "$618 → $1.12M profit." Never round numbers. Specificity sells.
9. **Roster scarcity** — "2 spots Q1" (Theriot), "10-20% acceptance" (Growth Ghost), "turn away more than we take" (Moonlighters), "limited roster" (Siba). Adopt from day 1.
10. **Frameworks > tactics in content** — Every educator-operator packages tactics into named frameworks (G-A-P, Quadrant Method, Hook Multiplier, FBI-level Research). Frameworks compound; tactics decay.
