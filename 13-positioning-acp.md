## 4. Message Hierarchy (say it in this order)

**FIRST — Show, don't tell (the reply-getter):**
> For priority prospects, the *first thing* is a made-for-them rendered sample — their product, their reviews, in a 15-sec UGC ad. This precedes all copy. It kills "AI is slop" and "you don't know my brand" simultaneously.

**SECOND — The Bridge insight (both camps nod):**
> Creative fatigue sets in at 7 weeks. Your winners die on a clock; the only defense is fresh variation faster than the fatigue. *(Camp A agrees but lacks production capacity; Camp B agrees and wants throughput.)*

**THIRD — The Mechanism (4×24, winner-framed):**
> 4 hypothesis angles → 24 variations per 2-week cycle, launched and tagged in your account, iterated live. Volume is *how the winner gets found faster*, not the product.

**FOURTH — Credibility + De-risk (proof-only; no claimed ad track record):**
> I'm newer to AI ad creative than the 10-year agencies — so the proof is the work, on your product, before you pay: the free made-for-you sample, a portfolio of rendered ads and published teardowns that show I understand *why* creative wins or dies, and a real hold-rate number off an ad I built and ran with my own small spend. I'm an operator, not an agency middleman — I built a real business of my own once, so I respect your margin. Claims gated to what you can substantiate. And: beat your control on hold-rate while CPA holds flat in 30 days, or next cycle's free; month-to-month, keep every video; I cap at 5 brands.

*Rule: never lead with price or with the word "AI." Lead with their sample (if priority), then the 7-week truth, then the mechanism, then proof-only credibility + guarantee. Never claim an ad track record you don't have.*

## 5. The 3 Proof Pillars *(revised — proof-only, no claimed ad track record)*

1. **Judgment (not generation), claim-gated, and *demonstrated*.** "ChatGPT writes a script. It can't tell you which of 4 angles leads for your $90-AOV SKU vs your $45 SKU — and it won't gate the health claim that gets your account banned." **Proof: published ad teardowns that dissect real functional-consumables ads (hook, hold-rate decay, the load-bearing claim, the angles I'd test) — demonstrating ad-anatomy judgment without needing a case study — plus a blind paid head-to-head vs the client's own buyer, and the substantiation gate itself.**
2. **Production-and-launch hands + live iteration.** 24 variations/2 wks, *launched and tagged in their account*, kill losers / double winners. Proof: the cadence is countable (24/2wks vs ~4/mo); the hold-rate + CPA-flat guarantee; deliverable is "launched," not "24 mp4s in a Drive folder."
3. **Proof-by-demonstration + hard risk reversal (in lieu of a track record).** I claim no ad history — I *show* the work. **Proof: the free made-for-them sample (one rendered ad of their own product before any payment — the #1 trust mechanism); a self-made portfolio of rendered videos; a real hold-rate number off an ad I built and ran with my own small spend; the founding-partner pilots that manufacture the first 3 documented deltas; and a guarantee that loads the risk onto me — month-to-month, keep every video, capped at 5 brands. The public proof ledger increments as real deltas land.** Confident framing: "newer than the 10-year agencies, so I prove it on your product first and put the risk on me" — never an apology, and never the unrelated business as the headline credential.

## 6. Tone / Voice Guide

- **Persona:** Real operator who built a business of his own and respects margin, now newer to AI ad creative and proving it on your product first instead of claiming a track record. Not a guru, not a hype-man, not an agency middleman.
- **Specificity is the brand.** Always concrete: `$20–100K/mo`, `24 variations`, `4 angles`, `2-week cycles`, `7-week fatigue`, `$90 AOV / $30 CPA / 3× ROAS`, `$497 pack`, `5-brand cap`. Never round-number vagueness.
- **$ and % on every claim that can carry one.** "Cheaper" → "$200–500 all-in per video, not weeks-and-hundreds per creator."
- **Plain declarative sentences.** No jargon stacking. "Ship 24 ads in 2 weeks," not "leverage synergistic AI workflows."
- **Earned empathy, never pity.** With A: "the game changed under you; here's the lever you couldn't pull," never "I know it's been hard."
- **Volume is never the hero.** Always frame as "how the winner gets found faster." Leading with volume pings the slop alarm.
- **Honest about limits.** Name the AI failure mode (consumption B-roll) before they do. Name the real client time cost. Honesty builds the belief the offer is short on.
- **Don't sell their community's grief back to them.** Reference Andromeda as shared context; don't paste a Reddit quote as a manipulative hook.
- **No emojis, no exclamation-point selling.** Confidence comes from specificity.
- **Use:** find the winner, angles, variations, iterate, kill losers/double winners, launched and tagged, P&L, auction, fatigue, hold-rate, claim-gated, capped at 5.
- **Avoid:** revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, synergy, death spiral, "got your nights back," outvariant, 10x-as-filler, "effortless," "near-zero effort."
