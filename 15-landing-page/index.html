What actually broke

You had a creative carrying the account. Then it stopped — for no reason you could find.

Sometime after mid-February last year it quit working. Same creative, same audience, double the CPMs, no explanation. You didn't change anything; it broke anyway. You'd already tried the targeting fixes, the budget restructures, the consolidation, all of it. None of it was the problem — because the problem was never targeting.

So now you ship maybe four tests a month and hope.
