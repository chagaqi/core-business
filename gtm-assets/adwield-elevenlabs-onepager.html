What we do

Adwield is an ad-creative engine for DTC functional-consumables brands (supplements, functional foods, performance nutrition). Every two weeks we ship a "loadout": 24 production-grade video ad variations across 4 strategic angles, launched and tagged directly in the brand's own ad account. We sell creative velocity, not files in a folder.

The problem

Winning ads fatigue around week 7. A lean brand can't produce tested replacements fast enough to stay ahead of it, and agencies are slow and expensive. Creative supply is now the real growth lever, and most brands are starved of it.

Where ElevenLabs fits

Voice is the layer that makes the volume possible. Every variation needs a voice, so we generate UGC-style avatar delivery, hook reads, and VSL narration at high volume — dozens of variations per cycle, across multiple brands. ElevenLabs turns per-video voiceover from a bottleneck into a near-zero-marginal-cost step. Grant credits go directly into shipped ad volume.

Why it works

  • In-account delivery. Launched and tagged where they buy, not a Drive folder.
  • 4×24 system. Structured angle testing, not one-off creative.
  • Operator judgment + AI. Brand context and selection by a human; volume by agents. The judgment is the moat against generic AI slop.
  • Proof-first. We build a free sample ad of the brand's own product before any contract.

Founder

Dylan. Built and ran a ~$2M/yr home-gym e-commerce brand end to end, including in-house fulfillment and customer support. Knows DTC operations from the inside. Adwield is run solo, augmented by a stack of AI agents that handle scripting, variation, rendering, and voiceover.

Use of voice (scaling)

Voice generation scales linearly with brands × variations × cycles. As Adwield onboards founding brands, ElevenLabs usage compounds: more ads, more languages, more avatar voices. Adwield is built to be a high-volume, recurring consumer of AI audio.
