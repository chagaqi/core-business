## PRODUCT INFO TAB

### 1. Product Description

Adwield is an AI-native ad-creative engine for DTC functional-consumables brands (supplements, functional foods, performance nutrition). Every two weeks we ship a "loadout": 24 production-grade video ad variations across 4 strategic angles, launched and tagged directly in the brand's own ad account. ElevenLabs is the voice layer of the entire pipeline. Every variation needs a voice, so we generate UGC-style avatar delivery, hook reads, and VSL narration at high volume across dozens of variations per cycle and multiple brands at once. Winning ads fatigue around week 7; our edge is shipping fresh, tested creative faster than a brand can in-house, and AI voice is what makes that volume economical instead of a per-video bottleneck.

### 2. Is your product already launched/live?

Yes, live and operational at adwield.com, with the full creative pipeline and methodology built. We are in early go-to-market: pre-first-paid-client by design, onboarding founding brands by building each a free sample ad of their own product before any contract. The product works today; we are in first-customer acquisition, leading with demonstrated creative rather than promises.

### 3. Tell us about your team / how many people

Solo founder, AI-augmented. I'm Dylan. I built and ran a ~$2M/year home-gym e-commerce brand end to end, including in-house fulfillment and customer support, so I understand DTC operations from the inside. Adwield runs lean by design: I own strategy, brand context, and the operator judgment that separates real creative from AI slop, while AI agents handle production volume (scripting, variation, rendering, voiceover via ElevenLabs). One operator ships the output of a full team. Today: one founder plus the agent stack, scaling with revenue.
