The problem

So you run maybe four tests a month and hope. You're not short on ideas — you're short on hands to produce the volume the auction already wants.

FAQ

Isn't this just AI slop?
The honest failure mode in this vertical is consumption "drink-and-react" B-roll — face-on sips that hit the uncanny valley. We route around it deliberately: product-forward framing, hands-and-pour over face-on-sip, voiceover over reaction. Functional consumables are product-centric, so the product is the star and the avatar just holds it. And we don't argue the point — the free First Strike lets you watch the quality on your own product before you decide.
Why would I trust a newer shop over a ten-year agency?
You shouldn't trust it on faith — that's the whole design. We prove it on your product first, free, with the risk reversed: beat your control or the next cycle's free, month-to-month, you keep every video. A ten-year agency asks you to trust a logo wall; we hand you a working ad and a guarantee. Newness is the reason the First Strike and the guarantee exist — not something to paper over.
How can you really make 24 ads off 4 approvals?
There's a review loop, not 24 surprises. You greenlight 4 angles and the specific claims each can make. We send the 4 hooks plus script directions per angle for sign-off before mass production — so the 24 are variations on concepts you already approved. Every variation passes a pre-launch QC checklist (render quality, mouth-sync, hands, brand-safety, claim language) before it ever touches your spend.
How much of my time does this actually take?
We won't insult you with "near-zero effort." Real cost: a one-time brand-context intake of about 2–3 hours, then roughly 30–45 minutes per cycle to approve angles, claims, and the concept lock. We own scripting, production, iteration, and the launch + tagging in your account. You greenlight; you don't babysit a vendor.
What does it cost?
The First Strike is free. The public paid entry is the Starter Loadout — $497 for a 10-video pack, sized to be an easy yes at under 2.5% of one month's spend. Retainer pricing is quoted live and scaled to your spend and SKU mix — we don't post it because it isn't one number. Media buying (Raid Tier) is earned after creative wins, never sold cold.
What if I run 90+ creatives a week already?
Then we're probably not your creative department — we'd be one angle-family of leverage, and we'll say so on the call. We cap at 5 retainer brands, stated publicly, because four angle-families per brand per cycle is the honest ceiling for full-judgment production. Past 5 we refer out. That cap is also your protection: you can't become the deprioritized account on a list with a published ceiling.
Do I own the work, and which vertical do you serve?
You keep every video, win or lose — and if you take the Done-With-You path, the Forge stays inside your domain for good. We serve functional consumables only: mushroom coffee, adaptogen gummies, functional beverages, greens, nootropic stacks, mushroom chocolates. "The mushroom-coffee specialist" beats "an AI ads guy" for a mushroom-coffee founder, so we stay deep instead of broad.
