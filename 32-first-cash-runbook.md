# FIRST-CASH RUNBOOK
### From "production-ready" to "$3‚Äì5K cash collected" ‚Äî solo founder, proof-only, 4√ó24, own-pipeline (Sora 2 + kie.ai)

**Scope of this pack:** the precise, ordered sequence to collect the **first $3‚Äì5K in cash** ‚Äî not MRR, not $20K, just real money in the Stripe account. This is the cash sprint that funds the rest. The bottleneck this pack is built around is **production skill** ‚Äî every step is designed to accelerate it, not assume it.

**The one rule that shapes everything:** A priority prospect never gets a cold email without a rendered ad *of their own product* already in it. The made-for-them sample is the proof ‚Äî there are no case studies. That makes **sample-production time the true rate-limiter**, which is why we practice on the 3 fictional brands first and only render real-prospect samples once the renders are good enough to send.

## 0. The gate before the runbook starts: "production-ready"

Do **not** start the outreach sequence until you can clear this self-test, because a rough render sent to a real A-tier founder burns a scarce lead permanently (only 24 A-tier brands exist).

**Production-ready = you can, on one of the 3 fictional brands:**
1. Mint a consistent Sora 2 character holding a pixel-perfect product (label doesn't warp across 4+ reused clips) ‚Äî per `28-sora2-consistent-character-workflow.md` ¬ß5-step.
2. Batch 5+ clips through kie.ai reusing the tagged character, changing only action + dialogue.
3. Caption/hook-card/export to 9:16 + 4:5, FTC-clean (no disease/enhancement claims, no fabricated testimonial).
4. Produce a 15-second spec ad in **‚â§20‚Äì30 min** start-to-export. (This number is load-bearing: the whole funnel's throughput rides on it ‚Äî see ¬ß2.)

**Done-when:** You show a render to 3 people who don't know it's AI and none of them say "that looks fake/AI." If they do, you are not production-ready ‚Äî keep practicing on fictional brands. This *is* the bottleneck; respect it.

## 1. The exact ordered sequence (each with a concrete done-when)

The order is dependency-driven. Steps 1‚Äì3 are build; 4‚Äì7 are the live motion. The long pole (email domain warmup, ~2‚Äì3 weeks) starts in parallel on Day 0 and is *not* on the critical path to first cash if you bridge with DMs (see ¬ß6 and Stall #1).

### STEP 1 ‚Äî Render the portfolio (proof-of-capability reel)
- **What:** Render **10‚Äì15 finished ads** from the 72 fictional-brand scripts in `19-creative-scripts.md` ‚Äî span all 3 brands (MycoMornings, ZenChews, DailyGreens24) and all 4 angles (PAS, FND, MECH, TRAN), and at least 3 formats (TH, VO, LF). This is also where your production skill crosses from "practice" to "portfolio."
- **Why first:** It's the only proof you can show before any client exists, it doubles as deliberate practice, and it builds the reusable per-brand character/product assets and brand-kit setups that make later real samples faster.
- **Done-when:** 10‚Äì15 ads, captioned, exported 9:16+4:5, FTC-clean, named `BRAND_ANGLE_HOOK_FORMAT_LEN_vNN`, sitting in a portfolio folder/landing page. Each one passes the ¬ß3.3 QA gate (lipsync locks on hook, no warped label, ingredient names pronounced right).

### STEP 2 ‚Äî Stand up the go-live links (the minimal stack)
- **What:** Deploy the landing page free, wire in a Stripe payment link ($497 pack), a Calendly booking link, and one sending channel. (Full stack spec in ¬ß3.)
- **Why second:** A reply with no place to book a call or pay = a dropped lead. The links must exist before the first send.
- **Done-when:** You can (a) open the public landing page, (b) click a Stripe link and reach a real $497 checkout, (c) book a test slot on Calendly, (d) confirm all `EDIT_ME`/`[FILL]` markers are resolved (real slot count, real email, real links ‚Äî no fabricated numbers anywhere).

### STEP 3 ‚Äî Render made-for-them samples for the top ~10 A-tier leads
- **What:** For the **top 10 A-tier brands**, render the specific made-for-them ad named in `22-prospect-personalization.md` (the angle/SKU is already chosen per brand, e.g. Kiala Carnival Candy taste-test, Spacegoods Rainbow Dust ritual, First Person Sunbeam founder-story). 8 of these already have shootable scripts in `26-prospect-sample-scripts.md`.
- **Why now:** This is the proof spine of every cold email. It's also the single biggest time cost in the whole runbook (~15‚Äì30 min each = ~3‚Äì5 hrs for 10). Templatize: reuse the fixed 15-sec spec format and brand-kit setup so each is a fast variation, not a custom build.
- **Done-when:** 10 rendered, FTC-clean samples, each hosted at a clean shareable `{{sampleLink}}` (host with view-analytics so you can see if it got watched). Protect these ‚Äî never send a rough one; re-render until it clears the "doesn't look AI" bar.

### STEP 4 ‚Äî Enrich emails + send the openers
- **What:** Run the 10 leads' founder names+domains through enrichment (every lead-list row is flagged `enrich` ‚Äî **never fabricate an email**). Paste the verified email + the rendered `{{sampleLink}}` into the pre-written opener from `22-prospect-personalization.md`. Send. Sequence the follow-ups (4-email cadence from `14-cold-email-sequence.md`), routed by Track (Wounded Vet vs Volume-Lean ‚Äî already assigned per lead).
- **Why:** This is the ignition. The opener is already written per brand; the only two missing fields are the verified email and the sample link.
- **Done-when:** 10 priority sends out with sample-in-email; follow-up sequence loaded; replies routing to an inbox you watch daily. (Parallel: as domains finish warming, widen to the rest of the 24 A-tier at 30‚Äì50/day.)

### STEP 5 ‚Äî Discovery calls
- **What:** Reply ‚Üí book ‚Üí run the 15-min discovery script in `18-sales-playbook.md`. The call's first job is **spend** (decides retainer vs pack-first branch); second is **Track** (Refuge vs Leverage). You are never cold ‚Äî they've already seen their own product as a UGC ad.
- **Why:** The call converts curiosity (the sample) into intent (commitment).
- **Done-when:** Each call ends in one of: (a) founding-partner pilot committed, (b) $497 pack sold, (c) retainer quoted + verbal yes, or (d) honest disqualify. Log the verdict.

### STEP 6 ‚Äî Close founding partners
- **What:** For fits, land the close: present the guarantee (beat their control on hook/hold-rate, CPA flat in 30 days, or next cycle free), state the *real* remaining founding-slot count, and branch the ask:
  - **$20‚Äì100K/mo spend** ‚Üí quote retainer live (mid $1.5‚Äì2.5K / standard $2.5‚Äì3.5K), offer founding terms (locked rate + the $0 instrumented 5-video pilot before any paid commitment).
  - **$5‚Äì20K/mo** ‚Üí put the **$497 pack** in their hand (foot-in-door, one-time).
  - Either way, capture the **control baseline before the pilot** (their current best ad's hook/hold-rate) ‚Äî no baseline = no case study.
- **Done-when:** Signed pilot agreement (`24-pilot-agreement-and-intake.md`) and/or a paid Stripe checkout completed. Baseline screenshot captured.

### STEP 7 ‚Äî Collect
- **What:** Take the money. $497 pack = immediate cash via the Stripe link. First retainer = invoice the first month up front (month-to-month, paid before the cycle ships). Any paid founding-partner fee = collected on signature.
- **Done-when:** **$3‚Äì5K cleared in Stripe.** That's the finish line of this pack.

## 2. Funnel math ‚Äî backward from $3‚Äì5K

### 2.1 The honest assumptions (falsify against real data after the first 50‚Äì100 sends)

These are the launch-stage (zero case studies) bands from `25-path-to-20k-model.md`. Plan with the **floor**, because proof inventory is empty.

| Stage | Range | Plan-with | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Priority send ‚Üí reply | 8‚Äì15% | **10%** | Sample-in-email + true hook beats generic 1‚Äì3%; no proof caps the ceiling |
| Reply ‚Üí call/pilot interest | 30‚Äì40% | **35%** | Many replies are "nice, not now" |
| Call ‚Üí pilot **or** $497 pack | 40‚Äì55% | **45%** | Free pilot is low-friction; pack is impulse-yes (<2.5% of monthly spend) |
| Pilot/pack ‚Üí first retainer | 25‚Äì40% | **30%** | Offer doc's own floor; lifts toward 40% only after case studies exist |

### 2.2 What it takes to clear $3‚Äì5K (revenue mix)

$3‚Äì5K **cash** (not MRR) is reachable *before* a single case study exists, because it doesn't require many retainers. Three honest mixes:

| Mix | Components | Cash |
|---|---|---|
| **A ‚Äî Pack-heavy (fastest, lowest-trust-dependency)** | 6 √ó $497 packs + 1 mid retainer first month ($2.0K) | **~$4,982** |
| **B ‚Äî Balanced (most likely)** | 4 √ó $497 packs + 1 standard retainer first month ($3.0K) | **~$4,988** |
| **C ‚Äî Retainer-light + paid pilot** | 2 √ó $497 packs + 1 mid retainer ($2.0K) + 1 paid founding-partner fee (~$1.0K) | **~$3,994** |

**Note on the $0 pilots:** The free instrumented pilots are *not* cash ‚Äî they're the proof-manufacturing engine for later. They convert to retainers at ~30% *after* the first cycle delivers a delta (~Day 45+). So the first $3‚Äì5K leans on **$497 packs + first-month retainer invoices**, with pilots seeded in parallel to feed the *next* tranche of cash.

### 2.3 The send/call/close volumes (Mix B, the planning case)

Work backward from **4 packs + 1 retainer = 5 closes** that produce cash. A pack closes off a call; a retainer closes off a call (often after a pilot, but the first-month invoice can land on the call's "standard retainer, founding rate" yes).

Treating all 5 as "call ‚Üí paid commitment" at the 45% call-conversion:

```
5 paid commitments  √∑ 0.45 (call ‚Üí pack/commit)   = ~11 qualified calls
11 calls            √∑ 0.35 (reply ‚Üí call)          = ~32 positive replies
32 replies          √∑ 0.10 (send ‚Üí reply)          = ~315 priority sample-backed sends
```

**Reality check against the lead list:** 315 sends > the 24 A-tier brands and even strains the full 79-brand list at one touch each. But each lead gets a **4-email sequence**, so 79 brands √ó 4 ‚âà **316 touches** ‚Äî almost exactly the requirement. **This means: to net $3‚Äì5K you must work essentially the entire A+B-tier list through the full sequence, and your top-10 A-tier samples must over-perform** (priority sample-backed sends reply closer to the 12‚Äì15% top of the band, not 10%). If the top-10 A-tier convert at the high end, you can hit $3‚Äì5K on **~120‚Äì180 sends** instead of 315.

**Honest planning numbers to carry:**
- **Optimistic (top-10 A-tier reply at ~15%, packs close well):** ~120‚Äì150 sends, ~6‚Äì8 calls ‚Üí $3‚Äì5K.
- **Plan-with (10% reply):** ~300‚Äì320 sends (the full sequenced list) ‚Üí $3‚Äì5K.
- **Pessimistic (proof gap bites, 7% reply / 25% pilot‚Üíretainer):** the list *starves* before $5K ‚Äî you reach ~$2‚Äì3K (mostly packs) and must replenish leads (+40 brands) to finish.

**The single biggest swing factor is sample quality** (reply rate) ‚Äî which loops straight back to production skill. Better renders = fewer sends needed for the same cash.

## 5. Founding-partner ‚Üí paid conversion path

The free pilot is the **proof factory**, not the cash. Here's how it becomes the first paid retainer + the case study:

1. **Offer the $0 instrumented pilot** (5 videos) only to a qualified $20‚Äì100K/mo fit on the call. Framing: "I'm taking a small number of founding partners; in exchange for the right to publish your before/after numbers and a one-line testimonial, you get founding terms." State the *real* slot count.
2. **Capture the control baseline FIRST** ‚Äî their current best ad's hook-rate + hold-rate, screenshotted, before anything ships. **This is the non-negotiable step**: no baseline = no case study = the pilot was wasted proof.
3. **Sign the pilot agreement** (`24-pilot-agreement-and-intake.md`) + run the brand-brief intake (`production-sop.md` ¬ß1) ‚Äî collect the SKU product-prompt once; it's reused every cycle.
4. **Deliver the cycle** ‚Äî run the 5 videos (or a full 24-pack for the strongest fit) through the 4√ó24 line; launch and tag in *their* account, *their* naming convention.
5. **Measure the delta at Day 14** ‚Äî control hold-rate ‚Üí winner hold-rate, CPA flat-or-better, head-to-head in their own dashboard. This delta is **case study #1** (e.g. "control 1.1% ‚Üí winner 1.9% hold-rate, +73%, CPA flat").
6. **Convert on the data** ‚Äî the retainer ask now has proof on the table: "Here's your delta. The retainer is where this runs every two weeks before fatigue kills the winner." Pilot‚Üíretainer ‚âà 30% pre-proof, drifting to 40% once you have 3 deltas. Invoice month 1 up front = recurring cash begins.
7. **Bank the proof** ‚Äî retire one `[CASE STUDY PLACEHOLDER]`, insert the real delta into the cold-email sequence and sell sheet. Each documented win lifts Perceived Likelihood (6‚Üí8) and re-rates the *whole* funnel's reply and close rates upward.

**Cash timing nuance:** Because the pilot is free and its delta lands ~Day 14 of the cycle, **pilot-driven retainer cash arrives later** than pack cash. So the first $3‚Äì5K leans on **packs + any founding-partner that signs a paid retainer directly off the call** (sample was strong enough that they skip the free pilot); the pilots feed the *second* wave.

### Top 3 things most likely to stall first cash (and the fix)

1. **Production skill / sample quality not over the bar (HIGHEST ‚Äî this is your stated bottleneck).** If renders read as "AI slop" ‚Äî warped labels, uncanny lipsync, drifting character ‚Äî reply rates collapse from 10% toward 2‚Äì4% and you'd need 800+ sends you don't have leads for. *Fix:* Do **not** start real-lead outreach until you clear the ¬ß0 production-ready gate on the fictional brands.
