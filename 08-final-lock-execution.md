## 5. Differentiation from Arcads / Creatify / HeyGen

**3 pillars:**

1. **Judgment** — Arcads gives you 50 avatars. I tell you which 3 to use for your offer, which hook framework to lead with, which CTA structure converts in your category. 24 variations across 4 hypotheses per 2-week test cycle.

2. **Volume + iteration loop** — 20-40 variations/week against your account. Watch Meta dashboard. Kill losers. Double down on winners. 8-10x the velocity of any in-house person.

3. **Proof-only credibility** — no ad track record claimed. (Supersedes the old "I ran my own DTC store / imported from China / ran my own ads" line, which was never true — see `27-credibility-and-trust.md`.) Trust is earned by demonstration: a free made-for-you sample of your own product before you pay, a self-made portfolio + ad teardowns that prove I read ads through your P&L and your buyer, real data from my own test spend, and the guarantee with the risk on me.
