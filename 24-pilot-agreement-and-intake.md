## The deal in one sentence

I run **one full 2-week creative cycle for your brand at no cost** — 4 angles, 24 production-grade
AI-UGC video variations, launched and tagged in your own ad account — and in exchange you let me
measure the result honestly and (if it works and you're happy) point to it as proof.

---

## What you get

- **One full 4×24 cycle, free.** 4 hypothesis angles × 24 video variations, built for *your* product
  from *your* reviews, voice, and offer.
- **Launched and tagged in your own ad account** — in your naming convention and ad-set structure,
  not 24 files dumped in a Drive folder you have to wire up yourself.
- **A clean before/after read.** Before we launch, I capture your current best ad's baseline
  (hook-rate / hold-rate + CPA). At Day 14 I show you the head-to-head: my winners vs. your control,
  with the actual numbers on the table. No screenshot I cherry-picked — your account, your metrics.
- **You keep every video. Forever.** Win, lose, or draw — all 24 are yours to keep running, re-cut,
  or shelve. No license expiry, no clawback.
- **A real operator picking your angles** — substantiation-gated, with a DTC P&L brain, not a prompt.

**Your cost for the pilot: $0.** No card on file, no "free trial that auto-charges." Free means free.

---

## What I ask in return

Only two things, and the second is conditional:

1. **Permission to measure and publish the delta.** I get to use the before/after performance
   numbers from this cycle as a case study. You choose the privacy level up front (pick one):
   - [ ] **Named** — "We ran a cycle for [BRAND]: control hold-rate X% → winner Y%, CPA flat." Logo OK.
   - [ ] **Anonymized** — "A mushroom-coffee brand at ~$[band]/mo: control X% → winner Y%, CPA flat."
     No name, no logo, no identifying detail.
   - **Either way:** I never publish numbers you haven't seen, and I never invent or round a number
     in your favor. If a metric didn't move, I don't claim it did.

2. **A short testimonial — *only if you're genuinely happy.*** One or two honest sentences about
   working with me. If the cycle underwhelms you, you owe me nothing here. I'd rather have your real
   "it was fine" than a glowing line you don't mean.

That's the whole trade. Your free cycle is the consideration; the right to publish the honest result
is mine.

---

## Scope — what's in, what's not

**In:** One 2-week cycle. 4 angles, 24 variations, scripting, AI-UGC production, QC, captioning,
compliance screen, launch + tagging in your account, a Day-14 read and decision log.

**Not in (this pilot):** Ongoing retainer work, media-buying/budget management beyond launching the
test, offer/landing-page/funnel work, or a second cycle. Those live in the paid tiers — we can talk
about them after Day 14 if you want, never before.

**Throughput is honest:** I cap at 5 brands total so no account gets deprioritized. You're one of
the founding 5. That cap is the point — you can't become the back-burner client.

Not "near-zero" — I won't insult you by pretending it's effortless. But I own scripting, production,
launch, tagging, and reading. You approve angles and claims; I do the work.

## The guarantee (yes, it covers the pilot too)

> **Within 30 days, my creative beats your current best ad on hook-rate / hold-rate (3-second +
> thumbstop) AND your CPA holds flat or better — measured against your own control, in your own
> account, with the head-to-head data on the table. If it doesn't, your next 24-variation cycle is
> free. Month-to-month, cancel anytime, and you keep every video either way.**

For a free pilot that means: if I miss the bar, I owe you *another* full cycle at no cost to make it
right. The currency is my labor, not your refund — there's nothing to refund, because you never paid.

---

## What I need access to (and what I don't)

To run this in *your* account, I'll need:

- **Ad-account access** via Meta Business Manager **partner/assigned access** — the proper way, scoped
  to your ad account only. **Not your password. Not admin over your whole Business Manager.** You can
  revoke it in two clicks, any time.
- **TikTok Ads access** the same way, if we're testing there.
- **Your assets and brand context** (the Brand Brief): product details, reviews, voice, past winners,
  and the claims you can actually substantiate.
- **Read access to the relevant metrics** so the before/after is measured, not guessed.

What I will **not** do: touch campaigns outside our test, change your budgets without telling you,
move money, or post anything from your accounts. I launch our test creative and read its numbers.
That's the whole footprint.

---

## Your data, your claims, your safety

- **Your data stays yours.** I use your account numbers only to (a) run the test and (b) report the
  delta at the privacy level you picked above. I don't sell, share, or hand your raw account data to
  anyone.
- **Claims are gated before anything renders.** During angle approval you tell me which claims you can
  back; I write only to those. No disease/cure language, no "clinically proven" without a study. This
  protects your account from a Meta ban or an FTC letter — it's part of the work, not a footnote.
- **Confidentiality both ways.** Anything non-public you share about your P&L, margins, or roadmap
  stays between us, published only as the anonymized/named delta you agreed to.

  we both walk away clean. No hard feelings, and the door stays open.
- **No auto-renew, no surprise upsell.** After Day 14, *you* decide if there's a next conversation.
  Silence means we're done; you owe nothing.
